Our Facilities.

Gym Equipment

Here at ECG we provide the latest and greatest in gym equipment.
  • Weightlifting Platforms
  • Over 150 Pieces of Hammer Strength Machines
  • A 200ft designated Free Weight Area
  • A 50ft built in Crossfit Box
  • 3 spin studios, who cater for up to 30 bikes each!
  • A Olympic Pool, with Sauna and Steam Rooms

Swimming Pool

We have a official Olympic size Pool. With seperate pool for dive training. We also host multiple sauna and steam rooms, with a sports physiotherapist on hand for after those hard sessions.

Spin Class

We have 3 fully functioning spin studios, we can cater up to 30 people in each class. Classes are held at regular intervals throughout the day!

Social / Soft Play:

Come and chill-out in our, kids zone. Let the kids blow off some steam in out soft play center, whilst you 'chillax' and enjoy a drink on us! We have Free Wifi available. Our center is fitted with CCTV to ensure your childs safety, with hands on 'activity coordinators' who ensure children are safe and enjoying themselves!
