About Us...

This is the best gym with the hardest and smartest working personal trainers . We offer a variety of sessions from sport specific training to those recovering from injury. We also have trainers who specialise in back, knee and osteoperosis rehab. With the latest gear from Primal Strength, we have over 130 pieces of equipment. With a recent refurb we are more than ready to return to action after the coronavirus!

Our trainers are some of the best in the World. Each has worked in many different background of fitness and bring great skills to the table! We pride ourselves as being part of the community and really do strive to get the whole family working out together!
